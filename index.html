About Me

I was born in Richmond, VA born on July 3rd of 1998. My parents both originated from Cambodia which borders Laos, Thailand, and Vietnam. I graduated from Hermitage High School and started at least one semester at J. Sargent Reynolds. My major is computer science and I am also trying to major in computer programming.

In junior year of my high school I got my first job at Carmax, which I had thought was a big step towards a great future. After 2 years I felt that I have accomplished nothing that I aspired to be. In late October a coworker of mine told me about a "Coding Bootcamp" that his friend just graduated from and he had an amazing experience. I needed to take the next step in my life and pursue my dreams.
